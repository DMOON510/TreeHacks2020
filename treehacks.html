ABOUT

Our website displays a real-time map that informs visitors on which galleries are better to visit based off of foot traffic. Musevue utilizes Google Cloud's Vision API technology to count the number of people in front of each exhibit using intermittent images of galleries. We created Musevue because we believe museums are important institutions for cultural education. We hope that our site can streamline visitors' experiences and make the art that they encounter more impactful.
